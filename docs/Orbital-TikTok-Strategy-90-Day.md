# Orbital Solver — 90-Day TikTok Launch Strategy

**Goal:** Build organic presence, test viral hooks, drive signups  
**Target:** High school & college students taking math courses  
**Platform Focus:** TikTok (primary), Instagram Reels (repurpose)

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1-30)

### Week 1-2: Account Setup & Content Testing

**Account Setup:**
- Username: `@orbitalsolver` or `@orbital.math`
- Bio: "AI solves your math homework step-by-step 🚀 Link in bio"
- Profile pic: Orbital logo
- Link: orbitalsolver.io

**Content Pillars (rotate through these):**
1. **Problem Solving** — Watch AI solve a problem in real-time
2. **Pain Point** — "POV: You've been stuck on this problem for 2 hours"
3. **Comparison** — "Photomath vs Orbital" (show video advantage)
4. **Education** — Quick math tips that showcase the product
5. **Relatable** — Student struggle memes with Orbital as the solution

**Posting Schedule:**
- 1-2 videos per day
- Best times: 6-9 AM, 12-2 PM, 7-10 PM (student hours)
- Test different times, double down on what works

**Week 1 Content (7 videos):**
| Day | Hook | Content |
|-----|------|---------|
| 1 | "This AI just did my calc homework" | Screen record of Orbital solving integral |
| 2 | "Teachers hate this one trick" | Derivative problem solved with video explanation |
| 3 | "POV: It's 2 AM and the homework is due at 8" | Relatable + show Orbital solving fast |
| 4 | "Why didn't this exist when I was failing calc" | Complex problem solved step-by-step |
| 5 | "Photomath could never" | Side-by-side: static steps vs video explanation |
| 6 | "Comment a problem, I'll solve it" | Engagement bait → show Orbital |
| 7 | "The math app they don't want you to know about" | Product showcase |

**Week 2 Content (7 videos):**
| Day | Hook | Content |
|-----|------|---------|
| 8 | "Solve your homework in 30 seconds" | Speed demonstration |
| 9 | "This is how I passed calculus" | Testimonial style |
| 10 | "Type your problem, get a video explanation" | How-to/tutorial |
| 11 | "When the professor says 'figure it out yourself'" | Relatable meme format |
| 12 | "I tested every math app so you don't have to" | Comparison/review style |
| 13 | "Finals week survival guide" | Listicle with Orbital as #1 |
| 14 | "Comment your hardest problem" | Engagement + showcase |

### Week 3-4: Analyze & Double Down

**Track These Metrics:**
- Views (aim for 1k+ average)
- Watch time (>50% = good hook)
- Comments & shares (viral indicators)
- Profile visits → Link clicks

**Identify Winners:**
- Which hooks got most views?
- Which content type got most engagement?
- What time performed best?

**Double Down:**
- Make 3-5 variations of your best performing video
- Same hook, different problem
- Same format, different angle

## Phase 2: Growth (Days 31-60)

### Content Strategy Shifts

**Proven Formats (do more of what worked):**
- Recreate top 3 videos with new problems
- Series content: "Calc problem of the day"
- Duets/replies to student struggle videos

**New Experiments:**
1. **Trending sounds** — Use viral audio with math twist
2. **Duets** — React to other math content
3. **Story time** — "How I went from failing to A in calc"
4. **Behind the scenes** — "How Orbital actually works"
5. **User submissions** — Solve follower problems on video

**Posting Increase:**
- 2 videos per day
- 1 proven format + 1 experiment

### Engagement Tactics

**Daily Tasks (15 min/day):**
- Reply to every comment
- Comment on 10 math/student videos
- Follow relevant accounts
- Engage with similar creators

**Hashtag Strategy:**
```
#mathtok #calculus #mathhomework #collegelife 
#studytok #homework #mathhelp #precalc #algebra
#studentlife #finals #midterms #mathclass
```

### Collaboration Outreach

**Find & DM these creators:**
- Math tutors on TikTok
- Student life accounts
- Study tip accounts
- College meme pages

**Pitch:**
> "Hey! Love your content. I built an AI that makes video explanations for math problems — would love to send you free access. If you like it, maybe we could collab?"

## Phase 3: Scale (Days 61-90)

### Paid Amplification

**Budget:** $500-1,000/month

**Strategy:**
- Only boost organic winners (videos that already got 10k+ views)
- Spark Ads (promote as regular TikTok, not ad)
- Target: 18-24, interests in education, college, math

**Ad Creative Testing:**
- Test 3-5 different hooks
- Kill losers fast (< 2% CTR)
- Scale winners ($20-50/day)

### Seasonal Pushes

**Key Dates to Target:**
| Date | Content Angle |
|------|---------------|
| Back to school (Aug) | "Your secret weapon this semester" |
| Midterms (Oct, Mar) | "Midterm survival mode" |
| Finals (Dec, May) | "Finals week emergency toolkit" |
| Spring semester (Jan) | "New semester, new strategy" |

### Referral/Viral Mechanics

**In-App Viral Loops:**
- "Share this video solution" button
- "Invite a friend, get 3 free videos"
- Watermark on free videos: "Made with Orbital"

**UGC Encouragement:**
- "Post your Orbital solution, tag us, win free subscription"
- Repost user content (free content + social proof)

## Content Production Workflow

### Batch Recording (2 hours/week)

**Setup:**
- Screen recording software (OBS or native)
- Phone for TikTok upload
- List of 10-15 problems to solve

**Batch Process:**
1. Record 7-10 screen captures of Orbital solving problems
2. Add hooks/text overlays in CapCut
3. Schedule posts or save as drafts
4. Done for the week

### Hook Templates (Copy & Modify)

**Problem Solving:**
- "Watch AI solve [problem type] in [X] seconds"
- "This is how you actually solve [topic]"
- "The answer to that problem you've been stuck on"

**Pain Point:**
- "POV: You've been staring at this problem for [X] hours"
- "When the textbook explanation makes no sense"
- "Me at 3 AM before the homework is due"

**Comparison:**
- "[Competitor] could never"
- "Why are you still using [old method]?"
- "I tested every math app and this one won"

**Engagement:**
- "Comment your hardest problem"
- "Bet I can solve it in under a minute"
- "Drop your homework below 👇"

**Curiosity:**
- "The app they don't want you to know about"
- "Why didn't anyone tell me about this sooner"
- "I wish I had this in college"

## The One Thing That Matters

**Consistency > Perfection**

Post every day. Most videos will flop. That's normal. You're looking for the 1 in 20 that hits. The algorithm rewards consistency — keep posting and the winners will emerge.

One viral video during finals week can bring 10,000 users overnight. That's the bet.
