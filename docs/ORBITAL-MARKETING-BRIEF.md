## 🎯 What Is Orbital?

**Orbital is an AI-powered math tutor that generates step-by-step VIDEO explanations for any math problem.**

- Student types a problem (or takes a photo)
- AI breaks it down into clear steps
- We generate a narrated video walkthrough in ~30 seconds
- Student ALSO gets text steps below the video (like PhotoMath, but better)

**The pitch:** "PhotoMath shows steps. Orbital shows a whole video."

---

## 🔥 Why It's Different

| Feature | PhotoMath | Orbital |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| Text steps | ✅ | ✅ |

| Animated math | ❌ | ✅ |

**Orbital gives you BOTH** — the quick text reference AND the deep video explanation. Same price point, 2x the value.

## 💰 Business Model

**Minutes-based tokens (not subscription):**

| Tier | Price | Minutes | Per Minute |
|------|-------|---------|------------|
| Starter | $2 | 10 min | $0.20 |
| Standard | $8 | 50 min | $0.16 |
| Pro | $15 | 120 min | $0.125 |

**Why minutes, not subscriptions:**
- Students don't want another monthly bill
- Buy before a test, use up minutes, buy more when needed
- Lower barrier to entry ($2 vs $10/month)
- We keep 87-92% margins

**Cost to us:** ~$0.016 per minute of video (AI + voice)

## 🎓 Target Audience

### Primary: College Students (18-24)
- Taking required math (calculus, statistics, algebra)
- Struggling or want to ace exams
- Already using PhotoMath, Chegg, Wolfram Alpha
- **Pain point:** "I can see the answer but I don't understand HOW"

### Secondary: High School Students (14-18)  
- AP classes, SAT/ACT prep
- Parents willing to pay for tutoring alternatives
- **Pain point:** "Tutors are $50/hour, I just need help with 3 problems"

### Tertiary: Adult Learners
- Going back to school
- Certification exams
- **Pain point:** "I feel dumb asking for help"

## 📱 Go-To-Market: TikTok First

### Why TikTok?
- Math content goes VIRAL (see: that guy who explains math problems)
- Short-form perfect for "watch me solve this" content
- Our videos ARE the content — we're literally generating TikToks
- Algorithm favors educational content
- Young demographic = our target market

### Content Strategy

**Content Pillar 1: "Let's Solve This" (40%)**
- Screen record of Orbital solving problems
- Start with famous/meme problems ("that viral equation")
- "This problem has 10M views on Twitter, let's solve it"
- Comment section = free problem suggestions

**Content Pillar 2: "Math Life" (30%)**
- Relatable student struggles
- "POV: It's 2am and you still don't understand limits"
- Duet/react to wrong math in movies/shows
- Behind the scenes of building Orbital

**Content Pillar 3: "Mind Blown" (20%)**
- Cool math facts
- Visual proofs
- "Did you know you can prove this with one line?"
- Bait for shares and saves

**Content Pillar 4: "User Wins" (10%)**
- Testimonials
- "A student DM'd me saying they got an A"
- Before/after grade improvements
- UGC reposts

### Posting Cadence
- **Launch week:** 3 posts/day
- **Ongoing:** 1-2 posts/day
- **Best times:** 6-9am, 12-2pm, 7-10pm (student schedules)

### Hashtags
- #mathhelp #mathtok #calculus #studytok #collegelife
- #studywithme #mathtutor #learnontiktok
- #orbitalsolver (branded)
